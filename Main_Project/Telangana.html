TELANGANA

Telangana, in southern India, boasts a unique culture and heritage with iconic places like Charminar and Golconda Fort. It embraces Telugu traditions, including classical dance forms like Kuchipudi, and is known for its spicy cuisine, epitomized by Hyderabadi Biryani. This state embodies a vibrant blend of tradition and modernity, showcasing India's rich cultural diversity.
