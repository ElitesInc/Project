Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh, sometimes referred to as U.P., is located in the eastern part of the national capital, Delhi, and is a region of immense cultural and historical significance. Its capital is Lucknow. The Gangetic basin and the Himalayas also add to its natural beauty, along with landmarks like the Taj Mahal and Agra Fort. The state's complex social structure and vibrant traditions make it a dynamic slice of India.
