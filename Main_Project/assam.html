ASSAM

Assam, in northeastern India, boasts a vibrant culture and lush landscapes. The Bihu festival, silk weaving, and tea production are cultural highlights, while historical sites like the Kamakhya Temple and wildlife sanctuaries like Kaziranga add to its richness. Assam's unique blend of heritage and nature makes it a compelling destination.
