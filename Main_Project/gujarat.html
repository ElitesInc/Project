GUJARAT

Gujarat, in western India, is a culturally rich state celebrated for its vibrant festivals, artistic traditions, and delectable vegetarian cuisine. As the birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi, it holds profound historical significance. From the ancient ruins of Lothal to the spiritual allure of Dwarka, Gujarat's heritage reflects a harmonious blend of tradition and modernity, making it a captivating part of India.
