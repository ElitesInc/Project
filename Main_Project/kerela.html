KERALA

Kerala, also known as "God's Own Country," is a culturally rich and vibrant state located in the southwestern part of the country. The state is renowned for its high literacy rate and impressive medical facilities. With the Arabian Sea to its western border, its coastal beaches are a delight for tourists, featuring attractions like sanctuaries and houseboats. The blend of natural and cultural beauty makes it a captivating gem within India.
