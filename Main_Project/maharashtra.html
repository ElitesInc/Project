MAHARASHTRA

Located in the western part of India, the state of Maharashtra is home to visionaries like Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and Dr. B.R. Ambedkar. Its capital, Mumbai, also serves as the economic capital of the country and is the hub of Bollywood, the film industry. Maharashtra has a rich cultural heritage and boasts scenic beauty found in the Western Ghats. It offers a unique blend of modernization and traditional aspects in every facet of life, providing a taste of both.
