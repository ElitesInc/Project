RAJASTHAN

Rajasthan, the "Land of Kings," is a northwestern state in India renowned for its rich culture and heritage. Its vibrant traditions are reflected in the colorful attire, folk music, and dance forms like Ghoomar and Kalbelia. The majestic forts and palaces, including the Amber Fort and City Palace in Jaipur, stand as a testament to the state's royal history and architectural grandeur. With a deep-rooted ethos of hospitality and valor, Rajasthan's culture and heritage offer a captivating journey through India's regal past.
