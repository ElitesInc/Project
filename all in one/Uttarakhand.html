UTTARAKHAND

Uttarakhand, often called the "Devabhumi" or "Land of the Gods" for its sacred sites and the source of the Ganges River, is a state nestled in the Himalayas known for its rich culture and stunning natural beauty. With traditional folk music, dance, vibrant festivals, and ancient temples, it harmoniously blends spirituality, tradition, and nature.
