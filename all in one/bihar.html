BIHAR

Nestled in the heart of India, Bihar boasts a culturally vibrant heritage rooted in its historical sites and diverse traditions. The ancient seat of enlightenment where Buddha attained nirvana in Bodh Gaya, and its role as home to Nalanda University, makes it a place of spiritual and intellectual prominence. Bihar's colorful festivals and rituals showcase its cultural vitality, making it a captivating blend of spirituality, art, and tradition.
