TAMIL NADU

Tamil Nadu, located in the southern part of India, boasts a rich cultural heritage steeped in history and tradition. It is known for its classical arts like Bharatanatyam dance and music. It is also renowned for its cuisine, offering a diverse variety of spicy delicacies and sweet jaggery-laden goods. The state's culture and heritage are a testament to its deep-rooted spirituality, artistic excellence, and unwavering pride in its Tamil identity.
