WEST BENGAL

West Bengal, in eastern India, is known for its diverse culture and heritage. With a dominant Bengali cultural identity, it boasts a rich literary tradition, epitomized by Nobel laureate Rabindranath Tagore, and vibrant festivals like Durga Puja. Kolkata, the capital city, is renowned for its arts and intellectual scene. The state's culinary delights include iconic sweets, and it has a legacy of political movements and leaders, making it culturally significant.
